Hi! Welcome to Sláinte-Link. Sláinte-Link is a Web3 secure medical records system, using blockchain technology, preventing any future ransomware or cyberattacks. Sláinte-link was inspired to be created when the HSE suffered a ransomware attack on May 14th 2021, causing the encryption of 80% of the HSE server, which prevented medical professionals from accessing patients' records. Sláinte-Link lets medical personnel to access and update their patients medical records at any time, and allow patients to see all of their medical data in one place while it still being secure.
